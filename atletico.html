Olivier Giroud goal: Chelsea striker scores amazing bicycle kick in Champions League vs. Atletico Madrid

The original call was that Giroud was offsides, but VAR overturned that ruling and declared it a goal after it was found that the ball hit Mario Hermoso of Atletico Madrid and not Chelsea's Mason Mount.
The ball being touched by Hermoso before the goal meant Giroud wasn't offside and played in by a teammate.

Giroud joined Chelsea in January 2018 from Arsenal for £18m. The move came six years after he joined the Premier League from Montpellier for £10.8m.
During his time at Stamford Bridge, Giroud has contributed 39 goals and helped the club win three major trophies – with his most recent being the Champions League in May.

Tropies Giorud won with Chelsea

  • FA Cup
  • Carabao
