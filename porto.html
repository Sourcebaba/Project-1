The Coffin from Hong Kong by James Hardly Chase

A mysterious voice from the telephone; a beautiful Chinese girl shot dead; rich old man with a troubled conscience; a private investigator involved in murder;
a Chinese prostitute who talked too much; and a coffin.
These are some of the intriguing ingredients of James Hadley Chase's splendid thriller. It is definitely a book to keep you awake long after your bedtime.

I was just about to shut down the office for the night when the telephone bell rang.
The time was ten minutes past six o'clock. It had been a dull, long and unprofitable day: no visitors, a mail I had dropped into the trash basket without even slitting an envelope, and now this first telephone call.
